## Mission

A law-enforcement-first global evidence ecosystem designed to protect children from exploitation while preserving:
- **Legal admissibility** of all evidence
- **Jurisdictional sovereignty** of every nation
- **Procedural integrity** that survives court challenges
- **Officer safety** and mental health
- **Public trust** through transparency

## What This System Is

- A **passive evidence correlation engine** that never interacts with criminals
- A **jurisdiction-aware routing system** that respects national sovereignty
- A **procedural guardrail framework** that prevents trial-killing mistakes
- A **victim protection platform** that prioritizes re-victimization prevention
- An **oversight architecture** that makes suppression impossible
- **NOT a surveillance system** — [see Surveillance Exclusion Statement](./docs/SURVEILLANCE_EXCLUSION.md)

## What This System Is NOT

- ❌ A surveillance tool
- ❌ A hacking platform
- ❌ A vigilante system
- ❌ An autonomous prosecution engine
- ❌ A content storage system
- ❌ A suspect profiling tool
- ❌ An expansion beyond CSAM

## Design Assumptions (Non-Negotiable)

These assumptions are foundational and cannot be reinterpreted:

1. Officers cannot violate local law to "get results"
2. Evidence must survive disclosure, defense challenge, and appeals
3. Jurisdictional limits are real and must be enforced by design
4. International cooperation is slow — design for that reality
5. Officers are overloaded, not malicious — reduce burden, don't add
6. Political pressure exists — create process-based immunity
7. High-profile cases stall unless forced by process — automate forcing

## Key Innovation: Failure-Proof by Design

> **If a mistake can invalidate a trial, the system must prevent the mistake from being possible.**

Not warn. Not recommend. **Prevent.**

See: [Failure Modes & Guardrails Specification](./specs/FAILURE_MODES.md)
