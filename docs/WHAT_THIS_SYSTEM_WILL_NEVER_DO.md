# What This System Will Never Do

> **A Statement for Lawmakers, Oversight Bodies, and the Public**  
> **Version:** 1.0  
> **Status:** Draft  
> **Last Updated:** 2026-01-17

---

## Purpose of This Document

This document exists to establish **permanent, non-negotiable boundaries** on the CHILD GUARDIANS system. These are not guidelines. They are red lines that cannot be crossed by any operator, administrator, or government.

If any of these boundaries are violated, the system has failed its mandate.

---

## The Fundamental Commitment

> **"This system does not create power. It removes places power can hide."**

CHILD GUARDIANS is designed to protect children and preserve justice — not to create surveillance infrastructure, expand state authority, or enable vigilantism.

---

## What This System Will NEVER Do

### 1. Content & Collection

| Prohibition | Rationale |
|-------------|-----------|
| ❌ **Never store raw CSAM content** | Evidence is the fingerprint, not the body. Hashes and metadata only. |
| ❌ **Never render illegal images to humans** | AI pre-filters before any human review. |
| ❌ **Never train AI models on illegal content** | Models are trained on structure, not content. |
| ❌ **Never download or retain illegal material** | Ephemeral processing in RAM only. |
| ❌ **Never scan private communications without warrant** | Lawful authority required for any access. |

### 2. Interaction & Engagement

| Prohibition | Rationale |
|-------------|-----------|
| ❌ **Never interact with suspects** | Zero posting, messaging, or engagement. |
| ❌ **Never authenticate to dark web services** | Read-only, passive observation only. |
| ❌ **Never conduct sting operations** | Outside system scope; requires human judgment. |
| ❌ **Never entrap or entice** | Violates due process; poisons evidence. |
| ❌ **Never alter state on any platform** | Observer, not participant. |

### 3. Identification & Targeting

| Prohibition | Rationale |
|-------------|-----------|
| ❌ **Never deanonymize users** | Civil liberties protection; creates liability. |
| ❌ **Never profile suspects** | System identifies material, not people. |
| ❌ **Never assign guilt** | Due process requires human judgment. |
| ❌ **Never target based on prediction** | Only known illegal material, not speculation. |
| ❌ **Never expand beyond CSAM** | Scope creep destroys public trust. |

### 4. Punishment & Enforcement

| Prohibition | Rationale |
|-------------|-----------|
| ❌ **Never punish autonomously** | Punishment requires courts and due process. |
| ❌ **Never issue takedowns without authority** | System recommends; authorities act. |
| ❌ **Never block users or content directly** | Enforcement is human-authorized. |
| ❌ **Never retaliate** | Justice, not vengeance. |
| ❌ **Never name-and-shame** | Due process protection. |

### 5. Evidence Handling

| Prohibition | Rationale |
|-------------|-----------|
| ❌ **Never delete evidence** | Only archival after retention period. |
| ❌ **Never alter evidence** | Append-only; corrections are recorded. |
| ❌ **Never hide evidence** | Multi-party escrow prevents suppression. |
| ❌ **Never bypass chain of custody** | Every touch logged and signed. |
| ❌ **Never export without validation** | Defense Attorney Simulator required. |

### 6. Jurisdiction & Sovereignty

| Prohibition | Rationale |
|-------------|-----------|
| ❌ **Never override national sovereignty** | Enforcement is local; evidence is global. |
| ❌ **Never share evidence with unauthorized parties** | Jurisdiction permission map enforced. |
| ❌ **Never bypass treaty requirements** | International law respected. |
| ❌ **Never create a global police force** | Coordination, not command. |
| ❌ **Never force action on any nation** | Recommendations, not mandates. |

### 7. Oversight & Accountability

| Prohibition | Rationale |
|-------------|-----------|
| ❌ **Never operate without oversight** | Watchdog is mandatory, not optional. |
| ❌ **Never suppress transparency reports** | Automatic generation; cannot be blocked. |
| ❌ **Never tamper with audit logs** | Append-only, cryptographically chained. |
| ❌ **Never disable the Watchdog unilaterally** | Requires supermajority to suspend. |
| ❌ **Never hide inactivity on cases** | Stalled cases are automatically surfaced. |

### 8. Scope & Mission

| Prohibition | Rationale |
|-------------|-----------|
| ❌ **Never expand beyond child protection** | Scope is CSAM only; no mission creep. |
| ❌ **Never add new crime categories without charter amendment** | Formal process required. |
| ❌ **Never become general surveillance** | Purpose-limited by design. |
| ❌ **Never serve commercial interests** | No monetization of system. |
| ❌ **Never be weaponized for political purposes** | Process-based, not discretionary. |

### 9. Victim Protection

| Prohibition | Rationale |
|-------------|-----------|
| ❌ **Never expose victim identity publicly** | Privacy and safety protection. |
| ❌ **Never contact victims directly** | Services handle notification. |
| ❌ **Never require victim participation** | Voluntary engagement only. |
| ❌ **Never re-traumatize through system design** | Trauma-aware by default. |
| ❌ **Never share victim details with media** | Strict confidentiality. |

### 10. Operator Safety

| Prohibition | Rationale |
|-------------|-----------|
| ❌ **Never expose operators to raw content** | AI pre-filter mandatory. |
| ❌ **Never exceed exposure limits** | Daily/weekly caps enforced. |
| ❌ **Never ignore wellness indicators** | Automatic support triggers. |
| ❌ **Never skip mandatory rotation** | Mental health protection. |
| ❌ **Never treat operators as expendable** | Duty of care is design principle. |

## Enforcement of These Boundaries

### How Violations Are Detected

| Mechanism | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| **Audit Logs** | Every action recorded, immutable |
| **Watchdog Monitoring** | Anomalies surfaced automatically |
| **Multi-Party Escrow** | No single point of control |
| **Transparency Reports** | Aggregate patterns visible |
| **External Audit** | Periodic third-party review |

### What Happens If a Boundary Is Violated

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Violation logged with full context |
| 2 | Watchdog alert generated |
| 3 | Oversight board notified |
| 4 | Public disclosure (aggregate) in transparency report |
| 5 | Remediation required before continued operation |

### Who Has Authority to Change These Boundaries

**No one can unilaterally change these boundaries.**

Any amendment requires:
1. Written proposal with rationale
2. Technical governance review
3. Legal advisory review
4. Public comment period (minimum 30 days)
5. Supermajority approval (≥75%)
6. Charter amendment publication

---

## Why These Boundaries Exist

### To Protect Children

The system exists to stop ongoing harm. If it becomes abusive, it fails children.

### To Protect Justice

Evidence must survive court. Systems that cut corners produce acquittals.

### To Protect Officers

Operators who are exposed to trauma, liability, or impossible situations cannot do their jobs.

### To Protect Civil Liberties

A tool that protects children but destroys privacy is not a solution — it's a different problem.

### To Protect Public Trust

Once the public believes the system is abused, its effectiveness ends. Trust is the operating system.

## A Note to Lawmakers

You may be asked to expand this system's scope. Before agreeing, consider:

| Question | Implication |
|----------|-------------|
| Does this stay within CSAM? | Scope creep destroys trust |
| Does this respect due process? | Shortcuts create appeals |
| Does this preserve sovereignty? | International law matters |
| Does this include oversight? | Unchecked power corrupts |
| Does this protect operators? | Burnout kills programs |

**If the answer to any of these is "no," the expansion should not proceed.**

## A Note to the Public

This system is designed to be boring, accountable, and limited.

It is not:
- A secret surveillance program
- A vigilante network
- An AI that "decides" who is guilty
- A replacement for courts, judges, or juries

It is:
- A tool that makes known illegal material harder to hide
- A system that preserves evidence for lawful prosecution
- An architecture that prevents high-profile cases from being buried
- A framework that protects victims from ongoing harm

**You should not have to trust the people operating this system. You only need to trust the design — which is public, auditable, and constrained.**

---

## Conclusion

These boundaries are not negotiable.

They exist because:
- Power without limits becomes abuse
- Good intentions without constraints become overreach
- Technology without accountability becomes weaponry

CHILD GUARDIANS is designed to protect children, preserve justice, and respect rights.

**If it ever stops doing any of those three things, it should be shut down.**
